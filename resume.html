ABOUT

Hello! My name is Maddie and I'm based in Florida.

I've been studying web development and design for the better part of a year through Treehouse. Before that, I was making a living in Washington, D.C. as a professional violinist and music teacher.

Throughout my journey as a web developer, I have discovered that my true calling in the tech field is web design. My goals are to provide clients with clean code and unique, eye-catching websites. Although I am mainly interested in learning about user experience and web design, I also have goals to learn some valuable backend languages.

Other interests of mine include traveling (favorite country so far has been New Zealand but I aim to visit many more countries), baking, hiking and gardening.
