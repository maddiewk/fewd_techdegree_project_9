ABOUT

I am currently a front-end web development student at Treehouse. After I finish here, I plan to go on to study UI/UX design.

Throughout this techdegree I have discovered that my true calling in the tech field is web design. My goals are to provide clients with clean, reusable code and beautiful, eye-catching websites. Although I am mainly interested in learning about user experience and web design, I also have goals to learn some backend languages.

Before I began my web development journey with Treehouse, I made my career with music. I have played the violin since I was eight and it was always my dream to play with an orchestra for the rest of my life. I majored in violin performance in college and right after graduation, I won a small regional orchestra job. After playing with them for five years, I began to realize that maybe I wanted something different in my career.

Then I found Treehouse! I am proud of myself for how far I've come since starting almost a year ago, and am excited for what the future holds.

When I'm not sitting at my computer, I love being outdoors hiking, biking, running, swimming, and gardening. When the weather doesn't allow me to be outside, I'll be inside playing with my cat Pippin, catching up on the many baking blogs I follow, and baking sourdough bread.
